Established on September 20, 2017, the company is engaged in accident survey and accident reporting services.

By setting standards to emphasize overall quality To bring in equipment and technology systems as tools for work To achieve maximum efficiency With a service area covering Both Bangkok and other provinces

Accident investigation

Provides accident survey services in the event of an accident involving an insured vehicle under the terms and conditions of the policy. Surveyors will arrive at the accident scene within 30 minutes, along with advice on repairs. Accident surveyors cover the entire country of Thailand. Modern technology and systems are used in the work, from finding the coordinates of surveyors in the nearest area to quickly arriving at the accident scene. This enables time and efficiency to be controlled to meet the company's standards.
